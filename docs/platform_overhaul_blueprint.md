# Lumino Platform Overhaul Blueprint

## Product Thesis
Lumino becomes a map-first field performance platform for canvassing organizations. Reps execute in the field, managers coach in real time, and the organization tracks performance, training, communication, and customer feedback from one system.

## Primary Roles
- Rep
- Team Leader
- Manager
- Admin / Ops
- Recruiter
- Partner

- Admin

## Product Pillars
### Field Ops
- Canvassing map with customizable pins
- Area drawing and assignment
- Lead records with notes, files, tasks, and appointment scheduling
- Weather overlays for storm-driven outreach
- Rep location relative to lead dispositions

### Performance
- Leaderboards across any KPI
- Competitions and incentives
- Badges, streaks, and recognitions
- Shared KPI definitions used across reports, competitions, assistant alerts, and profiles

### Social + Coaching
- Rich user profiles with badges, reports, and media
- Messaging with announcements, group threads, pins, mute, and org-chart auto groups
- Enzy assistant for alerts, nudges, and direct user outreach

### Enablement
- Media library with progress tracking
- Recruiting and contractor onboarding
- Digital business cards and customer surveys
